Unity Projects

Try or Die

Try or Die is a 3D First-Person Shooter game I made for Game Production 2 Main Evidence 2. It's a game where you are being chased by a massive enemy that does overwhelming damage and follows you wherever you go. The only way to gain the ability to kill it is to collect every power-up and kill all other enemies in the game.

Get Those Alien Things

Get Those Alien Things is a 2D top down shooter where you need to collect alien items in order to escape, but the caveat is that those items are protected by enemies. You need to defeat them in order to collect the items and escape.

Hallow's Death

Hallow's Death is a Halloween themed 2D game. In this game you must fight off the Halloween themed enemies for as long as you can and increase your kills.
